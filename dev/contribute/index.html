We value your interest in our project!

Below you will find a few ways how to contribute to the Mirror's Edge Archive.

Code
The Mirror's Edge Archive is open-source. We encourage people to commit to our project. Whether you are a complete stranger to the Mirror's Edge universe or a DICE employee, everyone is welcome to share their suggestions. All our code is available on GitHub.
Files
While browsing this website you may have noticed that we have a rather large archive of Mirror's Edge resources. You can read more about this archive here. Even though we are convinced that we have already collected almost everything that's to be collected, we would be pleased to be surprised by your addition. Click here to submit files to the archive.
Feedback & Suggestions
All forms of feedback are appreciated. If you've got any, we ask you to open a new issue on GitHub.
Security
If you'd like to inform us about a security vulernability or have other suggestions regarding a security matter, you can find more information in our security.txt.
Legal
You may access our legal documentation including terms of use, privacy & cookies, DMCA takedown requests and regulatory information using the respective links in the footer of this page.
