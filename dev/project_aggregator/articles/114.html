The Art of Mirror's Edge is coming this February


By Susana Polo on Jul 06, 2015 at 10:00a @NerdGerhl


Dark Horse Comics and Electronic Arts are teaming to bring fans an in-depth look at the gorgeous design of the Mirror's Edge games.

The oversized hardcover edition will be packed with art from the visually arresting games. Says Patrick Thorpe, editor of the book:

"Catalyst is quite simply one of the most distinctive and visually sophisticated video games of all time. It’s so beautiful that, if you weren’t so busy running for your life through it’s innovative cityscapes, it would be entirely arresting. It was important to us to capture its unique style in our design, while giving readers an exclusive, behind-the-scenes look at the creation of the striking world of Mirror’s Edge: Catalyst, complete with commentary from creators, character design, and stunning concept art."
