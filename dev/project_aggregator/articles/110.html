Mirror's Edge: Exordium #1 Preview: Have Faith

By Susana Polo @NerdGerhl on Aug 12, 2015 at 5:30p

Mirror's Edge returns next year with Mirror's Edge Catalyst. But while you want for that February release date to roll around, why not check out Mirror's Edge: Exordium, a prequel comic that will set the stage for Catalyst. Written by Christofer Emgård, the writer of Catalyst, and drawn by Mattias Häggström and Robert Sammelin, Exordium delves into the past history of everybody's favorite runner, Faith.

Here's a five page preview of Mirror's Edge: Exordium #1, including the cover (drawn by Robert Sammelin). The full issue will hit shelves and digital outlets on Sept. 9.
